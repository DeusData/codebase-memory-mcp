|----------|-------------|

| query wrapper artifacts → verdict computation | Raw proof evidence is translated into promotion-facing outcomes |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01 | T | `scripts/gdscript-proof.sh` manifest summary rendering | mitigate | Preserve wrapper-backed verdict computation and test exact pass/fail/incomplete wording from real manifest runs so summary text cannot drift away from computed outcomes. |
| T-04-02 | I | `aggregate-summary.md` | mitigate | Always emit bounded claim scope (`approved manifest corpus only; current commit only`) and `do-not-promote` for fail/incomplete so summaries do not overstate support. |
| T-04-03 | R | repo `summary.md` / `aggregate-summary.md` | mitigate | Keep `Final outcome`, assertion tables, and comparability issues beside the new verdict sections so maintainers can trace every promotion decision back to concrete evidence. |
</threat_model>
